Thoughts from behind the lens...

On July 4, we were honored to be asked to do a shoot for the City of San Francisco, capturing the fireworks over the Bay. The still images will be on display in the City Hall foyer throughout the month of September, and later in the X Gallery.

Of course, we couldn't resist a bit of amateur videography, given our spectacular vantage point from Skyline Boulevard. When we got back to the studio and watched it, we thought it looked even better in high speed with the traffic going across the bridge... enjoy!
